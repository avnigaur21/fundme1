NSF Seed Fund (Phase I)

National Science Foundation

Description

Awards up to $275,000 in non-dilutive capital to startups conducting Research and Development (R&D) with high technical risk. The goal is to prove feasibility and commercial potential.

This solicitation applies to startups working in Deep Tech, AI, Advanced Manufacturing, and Environmental Technologies. Successful Phase I awardees become eligible for Phase II funding (up to $1,000,000).

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a US-based small business (fewer than 500 employees).
  • Primary employment of the Principal Investigator (PI) must be with the small business.
  • Work must be performed in the United States.
  • Focus on unproven technology with commercial potential (not basic research).

Submission Requirements

Pitch deck (max 15 slides), Executive Summary, and Technical Proposal (Project Description).
